Step 1: Requestor and Patient Information

If the requestor is not the patient and the patient is over the age of 18, the patient is required to submit a Proxy Consent Form. in order to grant permission to the Cleveland Clinic MyConsult staff in discussing the patient's health information. In the absence of a proxy consent form, our staff is not able to provide any confidential patient information to anyone other than the patient receiving the second opinion.

Due to State Medical Board restrictions, we are not allowed to offer remote second opinions to patients whose mailing address is in one of the following locations: Guam and North Dakota.

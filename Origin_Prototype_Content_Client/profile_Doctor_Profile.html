Bio

Richard Merckle MD has been a surgeon for more than 25 years. The main focus of his work are minimally invasive endoscopic surgical methods. Surgery is his passion and he demands the highest quality of work from himself and his colleagues. Merckle MD is a master of all common techniques of hernia surgery (surgical removal of groin hernia), that is why he is able to offer the best operation technique which would be suitable for you. Alongside with the surgical removal of groin.

The main focus of scientific and clinical activity

Hernia surgery

  • Treatment of inguinal hernia: Shouldice technique, Lichtenstein operation method, minimally invasive hernia treatment using TAPP and TEP techniques
  • Umbilical and postoperative hernia (abdominal hernia)

Gastrointestinal surgery

  • Antireflux surgery (heartburn, diaphragmatic hernia)
  • Gallbladder removal
  • Resection of stomach and intestines in inflammatory and malignant diseases
  • Lysis of adhesions

Conservative and surgical treatment of obesity

  • Installation of gastric balloon
  • Gastric banding surgery
  • Sleeve gastrectomy
  • Gastric bypass surgery
